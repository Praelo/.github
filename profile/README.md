## Praelo

**Systems for data, automation, and observability—built with foresight.**

---

### What is Praelo?

Praelo is a collection of systems and tools focused on:

- **Data** — collection, transformation, and structured insight  
- **Automation** — orchestrating workflows and intelligent processes  
- **Observability** — understanding systems through metrics, logs, and events  

The goal is simple:

> Build systems that don’t just react—  
> but **anticipate, adapt, and act ahead of what’s next**.

---

### Focus Areas

- Infrastructure and networking (VLANs, routing, segmentation)
- Containerized platforms and shared services
- Event-driven automation and agent workflows
- Logging, monitoring, and system visibility
- Data pipelines and transformation

---

### Projects

Key repositories are organized by function:

- `infra`
- `platform`
- `automation`
- `observability`

---

### Philosophy

- Build with **clarity over complexity**
- Prefer **systems thinking over isolated tools**
- Design for **visibility, control, and evolution**
- Move from **reaction → understanding → foresight**

---

### Status

Praelo is an actively evolving environment serving as:

- a working homelab  
- a platform for experimentation  
- a foundation for future systems and tools
